# Nx Productivity Engineer - Monorepo Showcase Project

## Introduction

This exercise is an opportunity for you to showcase your expertise with Nx in a practical scenario. You can use a pre-existing project that fits the description or create a new one. During a project walkthrough, you will present your solution and explain your architectural decisions and thought processes.

## The Scenario: Monorepo Showcase

Set up an Nx monorepo to showcase your expertise.
You are free to choose the frameworks and or technologies for these applications.

## Areas to Showcase Your Expertise

### 1\. Monorepo Architecture

Showcase your approach to designing a scalable and logical library structure. Explain how you promote code reuse and enforce clear architectural boundaries.

### 2\. Developer Productivity & Automation

Demonstrate how you improve the developer experience. We're interested in your thought process for identifying and solving developer workflow bottlenecks through automation and the usage of Nx features & best practices

### 3\. Performance and CI/CD

Highlight your skills in optimizing pipeline performance. Show your approach to CI/CD, including the use of Nx Cloud.
Showcase how caching works in Nx, where are pitfalls, how to optimise and configure it.

## Project Walkthrough & Q/A Session

Please come prepared to:

1. Provide a link to your Git repository.
2. Walk us through your project's architecture, explaining the trade-offs and decisions you made.
3. Explain your CI/CD setup
4. Discuss how your setup would scale and handle future challenges.
5. Explain best practices you have implemented
6. Getting asked general questions around the JavaScript ecosystem & Nx
